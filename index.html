MadanText
日本語 English

MadanText is a text mining tool for Persian language developed by Hasan Khosravi and Kido Ishikawa

Functions

  • - Morphological analysis is performed, and the frequency of each data is shown in a frequency table and Word Cloud.
  • - The frequency of co-occurrence between words is represented as a numerical value or a diagram of the co-occurrence network.
  • - Analysis results can be downloaded in CSV or Excel and applied to PCA and Hierarchical Cluster Analysis
